WHO IS ALEX MITTLER?
Alex is a business owner, Entrepreneur, Author and Speaker. He spent over a decade of time at Apple, even before the iPhone came out. Now, Alex is a leading provider of software & hardware integrations with his IT Group, Global Engineering. Alex also has a passion for digital marketing. He has inspired and equipped thousands of up and coming business owners with the skills and strategies they need to create, build, and grow profitable businesses.
Alex started his journey to entrepreneurship as an innovator in the automotive industry, garnering the attention of famous media outlets like The SiliconRepublic with his no-holds-barred approach to entrepreneurship. After finding the love of his life in 2014, he and his future wife set off to build a profitable business quickly. Alex transformed his passion and love for technology into business. He enjoys the process of constant self-improvement and is set on a mission to touch as many lives as possible through his speaking and coaching engagements. His passion for everything he does results in excellence and has opened the ability to build the empire he has today.

Alex is also a Coach, Mentor and #funnelhacker at heart.

EARLY LIFE
Alex Mittler has been collecting computers since the age of 12. Heâd watch TV or listen to the radio, waiting for the moment to take them apart. Checking out computer components and building innovative things were the real entertainment. Despite his young age, Alex opted in to every technology he could find, simply to study the art and science of technology.

In high school Alex was a state champion race driver (karting), and then became a race driving coach during his high school seniors. He was also taking 3rd place in the country at the nationals that year. CAREER
In college, Alex had his first success as an engineer reselling computer hardware, and by writing his own code. This small business hobby became an obsession, and launched Alex into the world of computers, where he quickly became one of the top engineering minds in the world. Within a year of graduating college, he landed a job at Apple Inc. and spent several years working for "Uncle Steve".

Alex sold everything from e-scooters, to books, coupons, teddy bears, marketing services to consulting, technology, and software. He reached international business level partnering with many well known brands, winning several prizes and setting up his global brand.

The limitations and obstacles Alex encountered was the constant capital required to grow his businesses and bring them to life. This in turn became the birthplace of the idea to change the game of marketing. He taught himself a way to make his leads pay him for acquiring them (read that sentence again, its a game changer). It took a great leap of faith and use of all his online marketing and software development superpowers. From then on, the game would change forever...
