WHO IS ALEX MITTLER?
Alex is an Entrepreneur, Author and Speaker. A digital marketing consultant and tech coach, making his mark on the Internet through his popular brands. Alex has inspired and equipped thousands of up and coming business owners with the skills and strategies they need to create, build, and grow profitable online businesses.
Alex started his journey to entrepreneurship as an innovator in the automotive industry, garnering the attention of famous media outlets like The SiliconRepublic with his no-holds-barred approach to entrepreneurship. After finding the love of his life in 2014, he and his future wife Kanyu set off to build a profitable business quickly. Alex transformed his passion and love for internet marketing, apps and automotive into his first and second businesses. He enjoys the process of constant self-improvement and is set on a mission to touch as many lives as possible through his speaking and coaching engagements. His passion for everything he does results in excellence and has opened the ability to build the empire he has today.
Alex is also a Coach, Mentor and #funnelhacker at heart.

EARLY LIFE
Alex Mittler has been collecting junk mail since the age of 12. He’d watch TV or listen to the radio, waiting for the commercials because to him, going out and building innovative things were the real entertainment. Despite his young age, Alex opted in to every marketing message he could find, simply to study the art and science of business.

In high school Alex was a state champion race driver (karting), and then became a race driving coach during senior year. He was also taking 3rd place in the country at the nationals that year. He continued as a driver coach during his weekends, learning valuable lessons about competition, hard work, and the art of winning. He graduated as one of the youngest motorsport coaches nationally.
CAREER
In college, Alex had his first success as an online marketer reselling computer hardware, and by writing his own code. This small business hobby became an obsession, and launched Alex into the world of computers, where he quickly became one of the top engineering minds in the world. Within a year of graduating college, he landed a job at Apple Inc. and spent several years working for "Uncle Steve".

Alex sold everything from e-scooters, to books, coupons, teddy bears, marketing services, consulting, technology, and software. He reached international business level partnering with many well known brands, winning several prizes and setting up offices around the globe.

The limitations and obstacles Alex encountered with the constant capital required to grow his businesses and bring them to life, became the birthplace of the idea to change the game of marketing. Together with his mentor Russell Brunson, he is now making his leads pay him for acquiring them (read that sentence again, its a game changer) using his vast knowledge in online marketing, Russell's sales funnel software Clickfunnels and a great leap of faith. From then on, the game would change forever...
